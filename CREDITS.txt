Quickstep uses third-party open source software, which is bundled in the
third_party directory, specifically:

cJSON - An ultra-lightweight JSON parser in ANSI C. Copyright (c) 2009 by
Dave Gamble. Released under the MIT license.
http://cjson.sourceforge.net/

Intel PCM - A library for accessing hardware performance counters on Intel
CPUs. Copyright (c) 2009-2012 by Intel Corporation. Released under the BSD
license.
http://software.intel.com/en-us/articles/intel-performance-counter-monitor-a-better-way-to-measure-cpu-utilization

Protocol Buffers - An efficient and extensible data interchange format.
Copyright (c) 2008 by Google Inc. Released under the BSD license.
https://code.google.com/p/protobuf/

gperftools - Google performance tools, specifically tcmalloc, a very fast
thread-aware malloc replacement. Copyright (c) 2005 by Google Inc. Released
under the BSD license.
https://code.google.com/p/gperftools/
